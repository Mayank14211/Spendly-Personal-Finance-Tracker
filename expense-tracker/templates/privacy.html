Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your email address for account creation and expense data that you choose to log, such as amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions of your transactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide solely for the purpose of helping you track and understand your personal finances. Your expense information is used to generate summaries, category breakdowns, and spending patterns. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Spendly is a personal project and your data is stored locally on the server where the application is hosted. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, but please note that no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Do not enter sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers or bank account details.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with any third parties. The application uses Google Fonts for typography, which may collect standard web analytics. We do not integrate with any external financial services, payment processors, or data brokers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export or remove all your expense records through the application. If you wish to delete your account entirely, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us.
